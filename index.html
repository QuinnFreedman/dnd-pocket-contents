What's it got in its pocketses?

When the players in my D&D game kill a goblin or find a sleeping villager, there's always someone who says "I check its pockets. What do I find?" I usually didn't have an answer repared and got tired of letting my players down by punting and saying "Uhhh... there's nothing of value" or "There's 4 silver pieces," so I made this tool to generate random pocket contents (and other trinkets someone might be carying).

Items range from mundane (purely for flavor) to minor magical items (potions or curriosities, not "real magic items"). Some inspiration for items was taken from these three lists, along with the PHB and DMG.
